SARTH ENTRANCE
In Chapter 6 this is the only possible entrance to Sarth. You will need a map, which can only be found in Stellan's House in Eggley. To enter this you will need Stellan's Key, only found on a corpse lying in the middle of the crossroads North of Krondor.

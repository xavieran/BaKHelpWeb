SQUIRE PHILLIP
Squire Phillip will be here in Chapters Three and Six. As usual he will be willing to talk, if you have something to ask him about. For example, if you've been to the (closed) Fife and Laurel shop, he will talk about this. If you've been to Craig's House (in the South Sector), he will have something to say about Craig. He may have lots of other information, for all I know.

 

OBKHAR
Obkhar will be found here only in Chapter 7. James, Locky and Patrus have never met him before, but Gorath and Owyn may have saved his skin in Chapter Four. Obkhar will impart information relevant to the main quest of this Chapter. The Webmaster wonders: Will Obkhar be here, if Gorath and Owyn failed to release him from the Naphtha Mines in Chapter 4?

GRAN PETRUMH
 
In Chapter 6, you'll meet Petrumh here, fleeing from Malac's Cross, heading for her friend Ulam's house in Darkmoor. Unfortunately she has left her Ale Cask in the Queen's Row. If you fancy fighting your way through an army of Pantathians, you can retrieve the Cask from Ivan. Granny will reward you with Picklocks [4] and Fadamor's Formula [16].

 

LYSLE RIGGER [Does he really look like James?]
 
In Chapter 2 Lysle Rigger (James' double) has locked himself inside the Darkmoor Barn. In order to talk to him, you first have to go to Malac's Cross and talk to Ivan at the Inn, then talk to Petrumh, and then talk to Ivan again and ask him about "Double". Lysle will now talk to you. Afterwards you can go back to Krondor and get a Glazer's Guild Seal from Limm. Lysle is a player in the Nia's Quest Drama (see SETHANON Sector). He will tell you that he found a Skeletal Hand behind Hershel's house and sold it to Glover. NOTE: Lysle can only be talked to in Chapter 2, but he is not essential to solving Nia's Quest, which can also be performed in Chapters 3 and 6.

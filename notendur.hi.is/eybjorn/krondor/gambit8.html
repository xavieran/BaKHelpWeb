GAMBIT OF THE EIGHT
 [SPELL HOME] [ALPHA] [TABLE] [CATEGORY] [OWYN] [PATRUS]

Scroll Price = 450.0

Cost Range = 2 - 18
Duration = Cost * 10
Damage = Remaining Duration

Line of Sight = NO

TROLLS Chest in SE Dimwood
BUTTON Chest in MAC (Lower)

GAMBIT OF THE EIGHT creates a magical MINE on one square of the Combat Grid for a number of Rounds equal to 10 times the Cost. The mine, if stepped on, will do Damage equal to the remaining duration, and then disappear. Enemies can be forced to step onto the Mine with either Invitation or Winds of Eortis. The Characters will also suffer Damage if you're not careful, although the Spellcasters only suffer Half Damage.

Example: Cast GAMBIT at a Cost of 18 points. The Mine will last for 180 (18 x 10) Rounds. If an Enemy steps on the Mine 5 Rounds later, he will suffer 175 points of Damage.

No Enemies are Immune from this Spell. Some Enemies suffer less or more Damage:

PANTH TIANDNS Damage x 1.5
TROLLS Damage x 1.5
DREADS Damage x 0.5
MAKALA Damage x 0.5
TSURANI SPELLWEAVERS Damage x 0.5

CHANTY
In Chapter 1 you can talk to Chanty the Fisherman. He won't give you any relevant information, but he's obviously afraid. You can also talk to him in Chapter 2, but now he will have moved South, and will be found in a House near a Tombstone. He will speak wildly of his experiences, and give you the solution to two Moredhel Chests in the neighborhood: EYES, and RING. If you check the tombstone, you will find Chanty's fingernails (ugh!), nothing else.

HOUSE IN WOLFRAM (PATRUS)
Entering this House in Chapters 2 or 3 will trigger Combat #10. Killed, the Enemies will disappear, and if you re-enter the House, you will meet Patrus, and learn that the Enemies were illusionary. Patrus will by sort of apologetic, and offer you 200 Gold Sovereigns. However, there's no way you can re-enter the House and collect the reward!
 The 200 Gold Sovereigns are actually there in the game's data files, so I don't think Patrus is a Scoundrel. The Scoundrels here are the game's programmers, who obviously blundered here.

 

HALFGATE SISTERS' HOUSE
Gena and Andrea, two of five Halfgate sisters, live here. They can be approached in Chapters 2 and 3 only, being essential to the GRAIN QUEST. (See Temple of Dala.) They will offer you LEATHER LEGGINGS in exchance for a TUNING FORK. They will also tell you that their sister Larissa can give you a TUNING FORK.
If you're trying to solve the GRAIN Quest, you will need those LEATHER LEGGINGS. You may already have TWO Tuning Forks, in which case you won't mind giving one away. If you only have ONE Tuning Fork, you probably don't want to part with it, because it is very valuable whenever you run into Trolls. So you might want to get hold of Larissa's Tuning Fork [4], only good for Four Troll Fights.

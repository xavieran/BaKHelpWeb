ARLIE STEELSOUL
Arlie can only be approached in Chapter 3. If you've talked to the Duke of Romney, and listened to his plea, talking to Arlie will end the Guild War. (You may not want to end the Guild War too soon, however, because the extravagant prices in the Romney Shop will enable you to make lots of money!)

There are two ways of approaching Arlie's House:

  • Via the road. You will meet Podrich, Arlie's servant, who will ask you if you've brought Arlie's food. Ignore him. Note the empty Chest in Arlie's field. If you can obtain Poisoned Rations (or poison some using Coltari Poison), and place them in the Chest, they will get picked up after 24 hours. Arlie will now admit you.
  • If you stay as far as you can from the Road, you can enter Arlie's house from the back, but you'll have to navigate an unpleasant Trap (see below).
Either way Arlie will give you a copy of Chapel's Rmur n Whepuns [100]. If you now go back to Romney and talk to the Duke, he will reward you with 300 Gold Sovereigns and a Virtue Key.

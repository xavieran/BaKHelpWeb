CRAIG (Chapter 3 and later)
Poor Craig! Not only has he been deprived of his treasured Ironjaw Trap, but also run into some foul sorcery in the East Sector, which has turned him into an old man. (If you ask Phillip, he'll tell you that Craig is actually in his twenties.) If you bring back the Trap, Craig will reward you with a Ring of the Golden Way. As a bonus, the Fife and Laurel will open up! (See North Sector for Fife, East Sector for Ironjaw Trap.)

 

MORAEULF (Chapter 7)

If Gorath and Owyn managed to enter Harlech in Chapter 4, you will have seen Delekhan's son before. Here he can be tricked into revealing the location of the Waani, the magical artifact needed to destroy the Rift Machine.

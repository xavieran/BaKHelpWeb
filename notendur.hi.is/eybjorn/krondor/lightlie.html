AND THE LIGHT SHALL LIE
[SPELL HOME] [ALPHA] [TABLE] [CATEGORY] [OWYN] [PATRUS]

Scroll Price = 200.0

Cost = 15
Duration = 2 hours

Line of Sight = N/A

Only avalable from Cullich [80.0]

AND THE LIGHT SHALL LIE creates the illusion that the Caster is a Moredhel. This is only useful to Owyn in Chapter 4, making it possible for him and Gorath to reach Moraewulf in Harlech, without being attacked by the endless groups of enemies in the area.

WARNING: The Spell only lasts for 2 hours, which is equivalent to 30 Large Steps, 60 Medium Steps, 120 Small Steps. You will almost certainly need to Cast it more than once, if you want to get out of Harlech alive!

WINDS OF EORTIS summons a Whirlwind which pushes an Enemy away from the Caster a number of Combat Grid Squares equivalent to Cost. Useful for forcing an Enemy into a Trap.

NETHERMANDERS are IMMUNE to this Spell. Casting this Spell at a Nethermander may cause a graphic glitch.

CAT HOUSE
If the party tries to enter this house, a crazed cat will attack Gorath. The Moredhel will lose 15 Stamina Points.
 

HAPHRA THE MYSTIC
 You can talk to Haphra in Chapters 1, 2, 3 and 6, if you're willing to pay 50 Gold Sovereigns for the privilege, but beware: She is possessed by a Rusalka, so don't believe anything she says. The trick is to reject her prediction. This will have the effect of exorcising the Rusalka. Exit the house, and kill her. If you now re-enter Haphra's House, she will give you a true reading of the future, a very valuable clue.

 

MITCHEL WAYLANDER (Chapters 3 and 6)

Chapter 3: Mitchel is involved in a complex plot. If you've talked to Jason in the Black Sheep Tavern, and the proprietor of the Upturned Keg (in Sloop), it will indeed seem that he is responsible for the Black Sheep Tavern massacre, but he has actually been framed by a group of Nighthawks, who are waiting for him to emerge from his house. If you try to enter his house, the Nighthawks will attack you (Combat 28).
Chapter 6: If you've talked to Abbot Graves (in Malac's Cross), Mitchel will give you a Note, which will enable the Abbot to escape the Crawler's wrath. (See Malac's Cross sector.)

If the following tabulated data is meaningless to you, then obviously you're not very familiar with BAK. You need to go back to the Main Shop Page, in order to learn about the basic concepts of Shops and Prices.

The table below assumes familiarity with the basic conventions. Any abbreviations used should be obvious.

In the OTHER ITEMS Group there are 5 Special Items: Keshian Ale Cask, Bag of Grain, Practice Lute, Vapor Mask, and Weedwalkers. The Ale Cask and the Weedwalkers are sold by ONE SHOP only, the Practice Lute by three Shops. The other two items are not for sale ANYWHERE. All these items will, however, be bought by ANY Shop buying the Other Items Group.

The B indicates that a Special Item will be BOUGHT by the Shop indicated. For more data on Special Items Bought by Shops, refer to the SPECIAL ITEMS BOUGHT Page.

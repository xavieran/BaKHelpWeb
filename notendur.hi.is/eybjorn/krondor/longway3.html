The Main Quest of Chapter 3 is confined to Zones 3 and 4. Numerous new optional Combats may be encountered in Zones 1, 2, 5, and 7. Below, these are listed by Zone and Sector. Sectors containing NO NEW COMBATS will be skipped. The number in [Square Brackets] indicates the last Chapter in which the Combat can be fought.

Exploring those areas in Chapter 3, you may run into Combats not listed below. If you do, then these are Combats which have been carried over from Chapters 1 and 2. So please refer to
THE SPECIAL CHAPTER 1 PAGE, and THE SPECIAL CHAPTER 2 PAGE, for a listing of Combats first encountered in these Chapters, which will have carried over to Chapter 3, if unfinished.

For detailed information, please use the MAP WEB.

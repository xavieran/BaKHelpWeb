KOBOLDS
The Kobolds want you to bring them a suit of Grey Tower Plate. You may have an extra one to spare, but if you don't, and if you want to solve this quest, you'll have to go and buy one, or better, find one. If you now talk to Naddur, his clues should lead you towards Tyr-Sog. If your Scouting Skill is 45% or better, you can find the armor on the western bank of the river, just south of the Bridge west of Tyr-Sog (see relevant map). The reward is worth the trouble - you will gain 5 Points of Max Health and 5 Points of Max Stamina for all Characters.

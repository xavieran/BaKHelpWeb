SQUIRE PHILLIP
Phillip will be here in Chapter 1, and will answer some of your questions at the beginning of the game. He may answer some more questions after you've been around!

The Squire tends to travel a lot - he'll be close to the Temple of Dala in Chapter 2, and in Chapter 7 (Dimwood) you will discover that he is an important player in the grand scheme.

 

LAMUT GARRISON
If you talk to the men of the LaMut Garrison, you will initiate the Quest of the Stolen Ruby. Talk to Sumani at the LaMut Inn, and ask him about Grey Warriors and Thefts. The Ruby is in the possession of the scoundrel Isaac, who hangs out near Hawk's Hollow (see relevant map). He will, however, not admit to this, until you've visited Keifer Alescook in Loriel (see relevant map). Isaac is willing to part with the Ruby, but only if you can repair his sword. In order to do this you'll need a Weaponcraft Skill of no less than 45%. If you return the Ruby to the LaMut Garrison you will receive 100 Gold Sovereigns as a reward.

 

SUMANI
The Tsurani innkeeper has a lot to say (not only in Chapter 1).
He can also raise your Melee and Defense Skills by 5 points, if you want to spend 75 Gold.

Tip: Selecting EITHER of the Skills (only) will result in 7 points!
Selecting BOTH Skills (only) will result in 6 points.
In Chapter 3 (or later), Sumani will tell you an incomprehensible joke, but only if you've talked to dwarf Dubal first. You can now go to Questor's View and make Grimm laugh. (See Zone 2: Questor's View sector.)

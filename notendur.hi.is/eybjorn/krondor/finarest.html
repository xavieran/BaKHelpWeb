Brother Marc (Ch2+) [100.0]

If FINAL REST is cast at a dead Black Slayer, this will prevent him from Rising from the Dead after 8 (?) Rounds. [Generally, if you kill a Black Slayer near the end of a Combat, you won't need to worry about this.] Some Nighthawks can also Rise from the Dead, but only if they are accompanied by a Black Slayer. Such Nighthawk Ghouls will similarly succumb to FINAL REST.

Here's a rough table of Shops and the Item Groups they will buy. Most Shops carry items which belong to other Item Groups. These items will, of course, be bought by the Shop. I have indicated this with an asterisk (*). Example: Armengar. The Armengar Shop will buy all ARMor, all GEMs and all OTHer Items, as you can see from the table. Note the * under MOD and POT. This refers to the fact that some Modifiers and Potions are for sale here, i.e. Flame Root Oil, Naphtha, Sarigsbane, Silverthorn, Silverthorn Anti-Venom, Herbal Pack, Dalatail Milk, Restoratives. These Modifiers and Potions will be bought here, but NO OTHER Modifiers and Potions will be. You can not sell Althafain's Icer here, nor Redweed Brew.

Note that GEMs are the only item you can sell in ANY Shop.

The Shops are in the SHOP INDEX order, i.e. alphabetically by TOWN, except for out-of-town Shops, but I've had to truncate some names, in order to squeeze the table into the possible space.

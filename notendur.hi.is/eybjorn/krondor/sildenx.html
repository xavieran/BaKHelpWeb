TRAP
This Trap is really too simple.

 

ABUK
 
ABUK will be here at all times (at either of the two positions indicated). In Chapter 1 he will only talk. In Chapter 2 he will offer you a lesson in Lockpicking: 80 Gold Sovereigns will raise your Skill by 6-9 Points (depending on your Selections). In Chapter 3 Abuk has additional information for you, but he won't cough up, until you've talked to Joftaz at the Silden Inn. In Chapter 6 you can gain Lockpicking Skills again! For 70 Gold Sovereigns, your can raise your Skill by 5-7 points.

 

SILDEN
Don't overlook Silden, even if it's a bit off the Main Road. The Shop is interesting, the Tavern is important, and the Mist Devil will take you to the Temple of Eortis.

JOFTAZ at the Anchorhead Tavern.

Joftaz is generally not eager to talk, but in Chapter 3 he will hand out a Quest: Recover a Powder Bag stolen by the Crawler. James will easily recover Powder Bag [8] from the house next door. There's no need to give all 8 charges to Joftaz, so first make sure James is only holding Powder Bag [1]. This will satisfy Joftaz, who will impart information, and you will also have gained a Powder Bag with 7 charges. Abuk (above) will be eager to talk to you, after you've got Joftaz to open up.
Don't miss the guy at the Tavern who will give you Fadamor's Formula [6]!

 

THE MIST DEVIL
The Mist Devil is anchored in the Silden harbour. For 30 Gold Sovereigns she will take you to the Temple of Eortis, the first time you try. You won't be able to return until you've performed the Rusalki Quest.

 
TEMPLE OF EORTIS
Bless Type #1 - Price: 30 + 85% of Basic Price
Basic Curing Factor = N/A (see below)
Teleport = NO

When you first enter the Temple, Beyla will give you a Quest to perform: Kill all the Rusalki infesting the banks of the Romney river. When you leave the Temple you will be automatically cured of the Plague, if you have it (likely if you enter here in Chapter 2), but of nothing else. You will not be able to enter the Temple again, until you've performed the Quest.

There are 15 Rusalki: two groups of three, two groups of four, and lastly one Rusalka is inhabiting Haphra the Mystic. The Rusalki are tough opponents, and the Quest is not easy to perform unless your company is well equipped. It is essential for Owyn to be in command of the SKIN OF THE DRAGON Spell. The five combats are # 02-06 (see this sector, the Two Inns sector and the Sloop sector). Rusalki are generally easy to find after dark, but some of them are also about in the daytime. (Since they make night-time travel in the area hazardous, most Inns around here have jacked up their prices. The prices will automatically revert to normal, if you solve the Quest.)

If you return to the Temple of Eortis after the Quest is done, you can get cured of any condition for free. This includes the recovery of any lost Health and Stamina.

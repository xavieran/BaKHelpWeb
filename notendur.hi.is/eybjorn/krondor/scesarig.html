Line of Sight = N/A

Held by Owyn & Patrus

Dabeh's Fanciful Trinkets

SCENT OF SARIG allows the Caster to detect Trapped Chests. If a Chest is Trapped, and SCENT has been cast, you will be given the option of deactivating the trap, in which case your Lockpicking Skill will determine whether the attempt is successful. If unsuccessful, the Trap will explode, and cause Damage.

NOTE: All Locked/Trapped Chests have a Lock Number, and Trapped Chests have a Damage Number in addition. When trying to deactivate a Trapped Chest, the game compares the Lock Number of the Chest to the Lockpicking Skill of the best lockpicker in your company. If the Lockpicking Skill is higher than the Lock Number, the attempt will be successful. If not, all the Characters will suffer Damage equivalent to the Chest's Damage Number. For information on individual Trapped Chests, see MAP WEB.

Staffs call for some words of explanation:

  • Crystal Staff is a NON Item (Never Sold/Never Bought) and has a Basic Price of 0.00 - NOT included here.
  • Staff of Macros has a Basic Price of 2000.0 - even though it is not sold in any Shop. It is technically in stock at Dabeh's, but never available. It is included in the table below for the record only (and the cheaters!).
  • Wooden Staff is a non-magical Item, carrying no Charges. Lightning Staff[40] and Staff of Macros[50] are magical, carry Charges, and must be computed as such, if the Charges are less than maximum. Lightning Staff [0] and Staff of Macros [0] are "worthless" NON-Items, meaning that a Shop will only pay 0.01 for them. (They can be used to fight, though.)

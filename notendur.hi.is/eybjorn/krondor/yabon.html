JEREMY'S QUEST
Scribe Jeremy wants you to locate a Chest "just west of Hawk's Hollow, on the southern side of a small hill" and find out what it contains. If you do this, he will give you a copy of Thiful's Bird Migrations, reading which any character can add 5% to all his skills. This Quest may be solved at any time, in Chapters 1, 2, 3 and 6. The Chest in question is TRAPPED, and guarded by three Rogues. (See Hawk's Hollow map.)

The maps in this Zone are coded differently. Yellow Manna Trees are indicated by a PURPLE number showing the amount of Manna. Mounds of Dirt containing Manna are similarly indicated with BLUE numbers. Two Special Mounds of Dirt are GREEN and clickable. Empty Houses are GREY, Cache houses are GREEN, and Ambush Houses are YELLOW. Ambush Houses are only clickable, if they are Caches. The GREY numbers indicate Nethermander Corpses, showing the amount of Rations contained.

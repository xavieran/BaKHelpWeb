ORNO will infect you with the Plague (at 21%).
Since this is your first opportunity to get Plagued, here's a short guide to:

HOW TO CURE THE PLAGUE
 Orno infects all the Characters with 21% Plague. It will escalate by 1 point per hour. 24 hours later all the Characters will be 45% Plagued. As you get sicker, first your Stamina will decrease, and when this reaches ZERO, your Health will suffer (and all your Stats as well). Obviously you dont want this to happen! Here's what you can do:
GET CURED AT A TEMPLE. The closest Temple is the Temple of Ishap north of Loriel. By the time your Characters get there, they will be sicker, and then you have to pay for each of them to get cured. Every cure takes 7 hours, so the last one to get cured will be REALLY sick! But there's a better way:
MICHELE THE HEALER lives in Loriel. She will heal ALL the Characters if you pay her 25 Gold Sovereigns. This is MUCH cheaper than the Temple! But there's a better way:
HERBAL PACKS. If you have Herbal Packs, USE them, and your Characters will get cured in 21 hours (if they're 21% plagued). This will happen while you're travelling, but perhaps you'll find it more convenient to sleep it off. But there's a better way:
RESTORATIVES. The simplest method. Each Restorative Gulp will diminish the Plague % by 5 - meaning that you only need to consume 5 Restoratives to cure 21% Plague.

TRAP
Patrus pushes the Solid Crystal forward in order to shield Locklear from the Left Blaster. Now Locklear can push the Transparent Crystal forward, until he has cleared the Trap. (The Pole Current will shield him from the Right Blaster.)
 

CATAPULT

The Catapult is aimed at Kroldech's House in Raglam, but a Gear is missing. (There is a reason for this, which you can find out from the Engineer in Raglam, see below.) Find the Gear, replace it, and the Catapult will take care of Kroldech.

 

KROLDECH'S HOUSE
Talking to the Moredhel guard here, and two of the locals at the nearby Tavern, should alert you to the fact that Kroldech has the Battle Plans you're seeking. Kill Kroldech, using the Catapult (see above), and the Plans (Spynote #21) are yours. As a bonus you'll find 150 Gold Sovereigns and a Sword of Lims-Kragma [100].

 

ENGINEER'S HOUSE
In order to get information from the Engineer, Patrus' Barding Skill must be LOWER than 40%. Patrus will probably have to get drunk at the nearby Tavern in order to achieve this. As soon as he can play badly enough, the Engineer will let you in on his scheme, and tell you where to find the missing Catapult Gear.

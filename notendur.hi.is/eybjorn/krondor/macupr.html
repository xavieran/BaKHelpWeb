NADDUR BAN DOK
Entering the Mac Mordain Cadal you will meet a Dwarf, Naddur Ban Dok. He will tell you about the Brak Nurr, who has been plaguing the Dwarves. If you kill the Brak Nurr (Combat #05), Naddur will immediately reward you with 150 Gold Sovereigns. (Note that you can reach the Brak Nurr without running into any other enemies!)
If you've talked to Sumani (at the LaMut Inn) about Armorcraft, you can also ask Naddur about this. He can't improve your Armorcraft, but now you can ask him about Swords. He will, for a fee of 50 Gold Sovereigns, improve your Weaponcraft by 10 points (15 points, if selected).

FLARR'S HOUSE & WELL
Make sure you visit Flarr. He will offer you a drink from his Magical Well for 25 Gold Sovereigns. If you drink from the Well you will regain any lost Health/Stamina points, up to a maximum of 100 points. Good news indeed, and even better than you might think, because you don't have to pay! You can access the Well as often as you want, and the Sovereigns won't get subtracted from your Funds!

 

LORD LYTON
If you're travelling in this area in Chapter 2 or later, you will be severely irritated by Lord Lyton's Tax Collectors, West and East of Lyton. If you run into them, you can either pay 25 Gold Sovereigns, or Leave. If you Leave, you can either retreat, or go forward and fight them (Combats 18 [West] and 19 [East]). After the Combat the Enemies' Bodies will disappear (they can't be searched). The same Enemies will re-appear in 34 hours. (It should be noted that their Combat Grid Positions will change to the Positions where they last died.)

These guys can actually be avoided easily. Their combat perimeters are quite narrow, and since they stick to the Main Road, you can avoid them by staying as far from the Road as possible.

But there is a more interesting way to deal with this. Talk to Lord Lyton and activate a mini-Quest. Lyton wants you to bring him SIX Suits of Standard Kingdom Armor in GOOD condition (this means 70% or better). If you succeed, the Tax Collectors will go away and never bother you again. Lord Lyton will also reward you with a Virtue Key, and a Spynote (#15) with solutions to 3 Moredhel Chests in the neighbourhood.

 

GLOVER

If you visit the Lyton Inn while Nia's Quest is active, you will overhear talk about Glover. Glover is hiding away in a house next door to the Inn, and will sell you the Skeletal Hand for 150 Gold Sovereigns. (For a complete description of Nia's Quest, see Sethanon Sector.)

 

MAX FEEBER
 
Max Feeber is a nasty fellow. Not only has he mistreated Gran Petrumh, but also co-operated with the Enemy. And he's also trying to scare Nia away, so he can buy her land cheaply. In Chapter 2 you will find Glazer's Guild Seals in his Barn. You can't enter Romney without one of these. You will find a Burial Cloth in Max's House (Chapter 2 or later), if Nia's Quest is active. (For full description of Nia's Quest, go West to the Sethanon Sector.)

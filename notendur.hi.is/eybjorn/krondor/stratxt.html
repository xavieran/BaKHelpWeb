STRATEGIES OF TRADING

Meant as a handbook of the relative values of trade items, the work also put forth a number of theories about the nature of trading and offered suggestions on how to deal with difficult merchants who wished not to haggle. After having paged once through the work, he went back and made note of some of the maximum values he could expect to make off certain goods in their prime condition:

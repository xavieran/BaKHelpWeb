TEMPLE OF ISHAP (near Loriel)
Not to be confused with Temple of Ishap at Sarth, or Chapel of Ishap at Malac's Cross.
Bless Type #2 - Price: 25 + 70% of Basic Price
Basic Curing Factor = 55
Teleport = YES

 

MICHELE is a healer. If your Characters have caught the Plague, she will heal them all for 25 Gold Sovereigns (which is cheaper than the nearby temple of Ishap).
 

KEIFER ALESCOOK deals in Gems. He doesn't want to talk to you, but if you've accepted the Quest of the Stolen Ruby at LaMut Garrison, he will tell you who holds it.

PUG'S PRISON
Pug's Prison is surrounded by a magical barrier of Dhatsavan's making. Dhatsavan will refuse to release Pug, until you bring him the Cup of Rlnn Skrr. You will find the Cup in a heavily guarded house in the extreme South-East corner of the island. After bringing the Cup to Dhatsavan, click again on his pillar, and you will be teleported to the Prison. Pug has lost all his Spells. Use the Cup to transfer Owyn's Spells to him.

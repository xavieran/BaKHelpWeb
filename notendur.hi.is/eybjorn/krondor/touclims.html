TOUCH OF LIMS-KRAGMA
 [SPELL HOME] [ALPHA] [TABLE] [CATEGORY] [OWYN] [PATRUS]

Scroll Price = 900.0

Cost = 50
Damage = Death

Line of Sight = NO

Locked chest in MAC (Lower)

TOUCH OF LIMS-KRAGMA causes Instant Death to an Enemy. The Caster will move up to the Enemy and touch him. The Enemy will be pushed to the edge of the Combat Grid, and die.

Six enemies are Immune to TOUCH. If the Spell is cast at these Enemies, the Spellcaster will stay put, the Enemy will be pushed towards the edge of the Combat Grid nevertheless, but suffer no Damage. The following Enemies are Immune:

MAKALA IMMUNE
NAVON IMMUNE
NETHERMANDERS IMMUNE
NIGHTHAWKS IMMUNE
RUSALKI IMMUNE
SERVITOR OF LIMS-KRAGMA IMMUNE

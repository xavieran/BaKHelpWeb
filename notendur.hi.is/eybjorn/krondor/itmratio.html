If the following tabulated data is meaningless to you, then obviously you're not very familiar with BAK. You need to go back to the Main Shop Page, in order to learn about the basic concepts of Shops and Prices.

The table below assumes familiarity with the basic conventions. Any abbreviations used should be obvious.

The Index of Rations (below) differs from the other Item Indexes. Some explanations are needed:

  • Rations are for Sale in some Shops, but they are also for Sale in all Inns. Therefore the Inns have been added. Inns tend to be cheaper than Shops, as should be obvious from the table below. (Shops are written in CAPITALS.)
  • Although it is possible to HAGGLE in most Inns, an Inn will never BUY Rations (or anything else).
  • Days Rations (sic) are only Sold by Inns. Although they are practically equivalent to Rations [1], they are more expensive.
  • SPOILED and POISONED RATIONS. I have not bothered to include the data on these. Spoiled Rations can be bought in the Silden Inn - nowhere else. Poisoned Rations are not for Sale anywhere. These two will actually be BOUGHT by any Shop in the RAT Item Group. The prices, however, are too low to bother with. The Basic Price of Poisoned Rations [7] is 1.0, the Basic Price of Spoiled Rations [7] is 0.7. Children of the Green Heart will give you the BEST PRICE for these - 0.6 for Poisoned [7], 0.4 for Spoiled [7] - obviously not worth it!

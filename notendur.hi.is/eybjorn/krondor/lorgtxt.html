LORGAN'S JOURNAL

A journal of an unknown dwarf, the majority of the pages in the book were concerned with a creature which the author referred to alternately as a Braek Nuir, Braak Noor, and Brak Nurr. Some mention was also made about a secret chamber in the lower chambers of the Mac Mordain Cadal which once had been occupied by someone named Rhuargh.

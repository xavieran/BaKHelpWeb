THE LIFESTONE CHAMBER
The ultimate object of the game is to reach the Lifestone Chamber, and prevent Makala from ruining the Lifestone. The Chamber is protected by a magical shield, maintained by six Tsurani Spellweavers. You must descend into the lower caverns, find all of the Six, and kill them. The magical shield will disappear. Gorath will be left behind to guard the Aal Dragon. Owyn and Pug then go forth to meet Makala (or their maker).

A little known fact: Pug and Owyn, before they rush forth into the final combat, can actually retreat into the room west of the Lifestone Chamber. This is not possible unless they have a minimum of 90% Stealth (or 60% plus Weedwalkers), and they will have to cast Dragon's Breath as well. The contents of the three Chests found here are surprisingly insignificant - although the Restoratives might be useful.

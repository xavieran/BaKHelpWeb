GRIEF OF 1000 NIGHTS
[SPELL HOME] [ALPHA] [TABLE] [CATEGORY] [OWYN] [PATRUS]

Scroll Price = 300.0

Cost = 4 - 16
Duration = Cost * 2

Line of Sight = NO

Sethanon Cache
FARRIER Chest in NW Dimwood

GRIEF OF 1000 NIGHTS causes an oppenent to become so depressed that he is frozen for a number of rounds equal to twice the cost.

Example: Cast GRIEF at a Moredhel Warrior at the Minimum Cost (4). The Enemy will be unable to move and act for 8 Rounds.

Some Enemies are Immune to this Spell:

DREADS
MAKALA
NAVON
NETHERMANDERS
NIGHTHAWKS
PANTH TIANDNS
SHADES
TSURANI SPELLWEAVERS

WARNING: This Spell may cause the target to assume an embarrassing, or even obscene, stance. The Cave Giant below is a *very* extreme example:

PATRUS: "Is that a club you're clutching, or are you just happy to see me?"

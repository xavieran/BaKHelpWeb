LUROUGH'S FIRST COMPLANT

To the Priests of Kahooli,

As he has been exceptionally unkind to me in his own fashion this year, I would ask that Tabar, who is the proprietor of the Candlemaker's Grin, find himself begrieved by a plague of fireants who shall not abate in their numbers for one and twenty days.

Lurough,
Proprietor of the Dagger & Star Tavern

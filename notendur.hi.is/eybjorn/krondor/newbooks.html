NOTES on the READING and RE-READING of BOOKS

This is best explained with an example, so let's use ACCOUNTS:

ACCOUNTS OF THE SHAMATA GARRISON SHOPS: 1
CACHES: 0

One Reading: 6 hours
100: 25 days 0 hours

INFO: Redweed Brew


Click		 Assessment + 5% (Not Selected)
Assessment + 7.5% (Selected)
A

Subsequent: 20% Chance

Reading ACCOUNTS for the first time will permanently add 5% to your Assessment Skill, if not Selected. If the Skill is selected, 7.5% will be gained. Selection generally adds 50% to any Skill-raising effect.

Question: The game only shows whole-number Skill rises. How can a Skill rise by 7.5?
Answer: The game actually measures fractions of a Skill Point, by using Experience Points. 256 Experience Points equal 1 Skill Point. If a Skill rises by 7.5 Points, it will rise by 7 whole Points, plus 128 Experience Points.

At each subsequent Reading after the first one, you have a RANDOM CHANCE of gaining extra Experience Points - in this case 20% (1 out of 5). A simple formula determines the Points gained (if you're lucky): 100 minus Skill.

Example: If a Skill is 20%, the gain will be 100-20=80 (not selected). If selected, the gain will be 80*1.5=120. This, of course, means that Low Skills rise faster than High skills.

Reading Books takes Time, and Rations will be consumed accordingly (at midnight). If you intend to utilize all 100 Uses of a Book, first make sure you have enough Rations! In the above example, one Reading of ACCOUNTS takes 6 hours. A full 100 Readings takes 25 days! You could starve to death, if you're not careful. (100 Readings of Thiful's Bird Migrations takes 62.5 days!)

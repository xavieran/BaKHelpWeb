TRAP
This Trap is purely educational. Run straight towards the Chests, and note what happens when the Blast hits the Current between the Trap Poles. So now you know.

 

BABON'S HOSTEL in Questor's View
A tiny Quest can be performed here in Chapter 3 or later. Talk to Grimm and try to make him laugh. You can't, unless you go to the LaMut Inn, talk to Dubal, and then to Sumani. Sumani will tell you a joke. Back at the Hostel, you will now be able to make Grimm laugh. The reward is a paltry 80 Gold Sovereigns.

 

HOUSE (in Questor's View)
The people here are extremely friendly, and really want to give you some food. If you accept the offer, Loralyn will give each Character Rations [2]. No charge.

 

TAD QUESTOR (in Questor's View)
Tad can give you a lesson in swordsmanship. If you're willing to pay him 75 Gold Sovereigns, all your Characters' Melee Skills will go up 5 points. Tip: If Melee Skill (only) is Selected, it will go up 7 points!

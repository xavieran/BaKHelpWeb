Below is a list of all items in the game. I have ordered them by Item Groups. It should be noted that although I've given these Groups arbitrary names, they are VALID in the game - Shops buy different Item Groups. Pay attention to the three-letter Item Group Abbreviations, as they are used in the Shop Pages.

All Basic Prices are listed. The Basic Price is based on the Condition Percentage/Number of Charges shown as [X]. Items shown in RED are STACKABLE Items. The (X) number shown for these items is the Maximum Stack Value.

Here are two examples illustrating the difference between Stackable and Non-Stackable Items:

Clerical Oilcloth is a Non-Stackable Item. Let's assume you have Clerical Oilcloth [2] in your inventory, and then find or buy another Clerical Oilcloth [2]. You will now have TWO Clerical Oilcloths [2] - not ONE Clerical Oilcloth [4]. This also means that you can not SHARE the Item.

Naphtha is Stackable. In the example above you would end up with Naphtha [4]. Naphtha can be SHARED. This means that if you try to give your Naphtha [4] to another Character (or, say, put it in a BAG), the game will allow you to decide how many Charges you want to give.

Does this matter? Yes, but only if you want to utilize a BUG in the game in order to cheat!

Rations are Stackable up to [14]. Day's Rations are equivalent to Rations [1] and will get added to the Ordinary Rations stack. Spoiled and Poisoned Rations are separate Items, will not get added to a stack of Ordinary Rations, and will not be consumed unless there are no Ordinary Rations available.

Bowstrings belong to TWO Item Groups: CROssbows and OTHer Items.

NON-Saleable Items all have a Basic Price of 0.0, and can not be bought or sold. However, I've discovered that if you force the game to sell or buy these items, the price will always be 0.1 - the game refuses to accept 0.0 as a price, and replaces it with the lowest possible price.

So why am I mentioning this? There's a reason:

One of the Non-Saleable Items can actually be bought (and sold), but only in version 1.01! In the JEWELS, KEYS, RINGS Shop in Kenting Rush you can buy a RORIC'S SEAL for 0.1! Not so in version 1.02. What a shame.

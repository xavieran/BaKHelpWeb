RIFT MACHINE
The Rift Machine will only be here in Chapter 7. It signals the end of the Chapter, if you have the WAANI. In order to get hold of the WAANI, you will first have to find the VICTORY Chest in the SW Sector, and then locate Squire Phillip in the NW Sector.

NOTE: Part of the Z-shaped hill north of the rivers' confluence is illusionary - you can walk right through it!
This is indicated clearly in the map above.

 

TRAP
This TRAP is rather confusing. You can encounter it with three Characters (Chapter 1,2,3,7) or two Characters (Chapter 6). The suggested solution below involves James, Locklear and Patrus in Chapter 7 (the only Chapter where you need to enter the area).

James can navigate this Trap all on his own,
without any help from his pals.
Figure it out.

 

THE FIFE AND LAUREL
The only Shop in Dimwood is absolutely CLOSED in Chapters 1 and 2. Chapter 3 is the first chance of getting it opened. Two events will trigger this - the killing of the Pantathians lurking in the East Sector, or the retrieving of Craig's Ironjaw Traps. If you kill the Serpent Men, you will be sure to find the Traps in a Chest nearby (see East Sector).

See South Sector for the location of Craig's House.

SHRINE OF ASTALON
Bless Type #1 - Price: 20 + 80% of Basic Price
Basic Curing Factor = 70
Teleport = In Chapter 3. Also in Chapter 6 (but see below).

You can not use this Temple's Teleport service until Chapter 3. In Chapter 6 the Highcastle/Northwarden area is out of bounds, so you can not Teleport to the Temples of Dala and Tith. There is, however, a bug in version 1.01 which allows you to do so! (For a description of the strange results of this refer to Chapter 6 Walkthrough.)

 

KRONDOR PALACE
In Chapters 1 and 2, the Palace can only be entered via the Sewers. In Chapter 3 you can enter the Palace directly, but the Sewers will be out of bounds (you can talk to Limm, though). In Chapter 6 you can enter the Palace both ways.

At the beginning of Chapter 2 you can enter Locklear's Room here, and "steal" all the stuff he had at the end of Chapter 1. This room will be available through Chapter 3. It is a "safe" cache.

OBKHAR
Obkhar is waiting for you to give him a Vapor Mask. That done, make sure Gorath and Owyn both have Masks, then jump into the river which runs through the Western Room. (Thank Ishap that the bloated Venutrier never got any closer to Owyn.) Having saved Obkhar, you can collect your reward from Irmelyn at the Armengar Tavern.

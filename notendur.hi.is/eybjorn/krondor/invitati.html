INVITATION
[SPELL HOME] [ALPHA] [TABLE] [CATEGORY] [OWYN] [PATRUS]

Scroll Price = 50.0

Cost = 1 - 10
Distance = Cost

Line of Sight = NO

Held by Owyn & Patrus

Dabeh's Fanciful Trinkets

INVITATION causes an Enemy to be pulled toward the Caster a number of Combat Grid Squares equal to Cost, and can be useful in many ways:

Prevent an Enemy from Retreating
Prevent Spellcasters from Casting by pulling them next to you
Prevent Archers from Shooting by pulling them next to you
Pull an Enemy into Trap Damage
Pull an Enemy onto Gambit of the Eight
Pull an Enemy onto Wrath of Killian

Three Enemies are Immune to INVITATION:

MAKALA IMMUNE
NETHERMANDERS IMMUNE
TSURANI SPELLWEAVERS IMMUNE

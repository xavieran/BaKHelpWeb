Common vs. Rare:
Many special Inn Dialogues, i.e. Chapter-restricted and Quest-triggered ones, will be found in the Chapter and Zone pages. All others are generated randomly, some being "common", while others are "rare". A "common" dialogue is one regularly encountered in most Inns. "Rare" dialogues are much less frequent, and some may be rarer than others, being further restricted by location and chapter. 		Man vs. Patron:
Generally, a Patron is a person with a "special" type of Dialogue, including NPCs (see NPC pages) and Gamblers. The Patron type of Dialogue shown below indicates that the Patron, although he won't tell you anything of importance at the moment, will definitely have more to say at another stage of the game (dependent on Chapter or Quest).

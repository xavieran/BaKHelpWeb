THE GREEN NUMBERS
Here's an example (Dragon Plate Armor):

Enemies: 95 - Caches: 6 - Shops: 5
This means that 95 Enemies carry this type of Armor,
it can be found in 6 Caches, and 5 Shops have it for sale.

40 - 2 - 50
These three numbers are derived from the game's data files, and are all believed to be related to the dulling of Armor. The first number (10-95) and the second number (1-6) probably indicate the likelihood of Armor being dulled, and the rate of dulling (by no means verified). High numbers seem to indicate rapid dulling. The third number is almost certainly the lowest percentage an Armor can reach.

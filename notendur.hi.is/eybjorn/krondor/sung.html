TEMPLE OF SUNG
Bless Type #1 - Price: 25 + 75% of Basic Price
Basic Curing Factor = 20
Teleport = YES

This temple's services are not available unless you first kill the "dream-sender", who is none other than NAGO, hiding out in Rowe's Barn nearby. After you've killed Nago, you should visit the temple again. You will be rewarded with Hocho's Haven.

A special feature of the Temple of Sung is that Health and Stamina can be cured here. The price is silly - 1 Silver Royal per each lost point.

 

NAGO
Nago and his three friends are waiting for you in Rowe's Barn (see Sarth sector). This can be a tough combat indeed, especially if you tackle it in Chapter 1, but Nago will still be patiently waiting here for you in Chapter 6.

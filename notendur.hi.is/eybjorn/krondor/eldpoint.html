LARISSA HALFGATE'S HOUSE & BARN
Larissa, one of the five Halfgate sisters, won't be here in Chapter 1, but in Chapters 2 and 3 she will ask you to open her Barn door, which has rusted shut. You will need Strength 30 or more in order to force the door open. If you succeed, Larissa will reward you with:

  • Tuning Fork [4]
  • Shell [100]
  • Herbal Pack [12]
If you're trying to perform the Grain Quest, and already have some valuable Tuning Forks you don't really want to give away, this Fork is not a big loss.

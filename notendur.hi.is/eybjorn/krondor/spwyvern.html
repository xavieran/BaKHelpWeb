Speculations: Bulldrakes (Yellow) are HOT, thus immune to HOT spells, but sensitive to COLD spells. Grandsires (Blue) are COLD, insensitive to COLD spells, very sensitive to HOT spells (compare Rime Giants). Hatchlings (Dungeons only) can only live Underground, and are thus especially sensitive to Earthquakes (Wrath, compare Brak Nurr, who is an Earth creature and thus immune).

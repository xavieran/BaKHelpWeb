Letter from Brother Jeremy

Of the fairy chests we have cataloged so far, Lord Lyton, we have found the following words can be used to open certain chests in your region:
River
Sword
Ice
It is our sincerest hope that in the future we will further be able to decode others as we find them. I shall send word by post if I discover anything of interest.

Brother Jeremy the Scribe

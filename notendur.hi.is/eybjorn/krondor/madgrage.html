MAD GOD'S RAGE
 [SPELL HOME] [ALPHA] [TABLE] [CATEGORY] [OWYN] [PATRUS]

Scroll Price = 1000.0

Cost = Special
Damage = Special

Line of Sight = NO

Trapped Chest S Elvandar
LIFE Chest in Valheru Ruins
LOGS Chest in NW Dimwood (Ch. 7)

MAD GOD'S RAGE calls down multiple Bolts of Lightning, which will continue striking the Enemies randomly, until they're all dead. The Cost of each strike is 3 points of Health/Stamina, so make sure you're Spellcaster is in good shape - otherwise he may be first to die! The Damage caused by each strike seems to fall randomly between 15 and 25 points.

NOTE: The Enemy must be Above Ground. This Spell is useless in Dungeons. Nethermanders are the only surface Enemies Immune to this Spell; others are Immune, since they will only be encountered in Dungeons:

BRAK NURRS
CAVE GIANTS
DREADS

MAKALA

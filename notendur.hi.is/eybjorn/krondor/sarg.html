LIALLAN
Liallan has a lot of information for Gorath.

If you've read the Spynote found on the impaled corpse outside Sar-Sargoth,
she will also give you directions towards Nalar's Rib.

 

NALAR'S RIB
If you've read the Spynote from the impaled corpse outside Sar-Sargoth, and have an Emerald, you can activate Nalar's Rib. The power unleashed from the Rib will eliminate numerous Enemy Groups near Sar-Sargoth. This will certainly make life easier in the area, and as a bonus you can freely plunder the corpses, if you can find them all!

Don't underestimate Nalar's Rib. It will eliminate 19 Combats = 98 Enemies! You probably won't be able to avoid at least three of these Combats, before you reach the Rib, but as soon as you do, you can start plundering and selling.

The problem is - how to find all the dead enemies! This is not exactly easy. Even if you're familiar with the Combat Positions as shown in the Map above, the Nalar's Rib Blast will seriously displace many enemies.

The location of all dead enemies after Nalar's Blast is too complex to be shown on the above Section Map. A Special Map is needed. Click the Rib (left) in order to view this.

 

SORCERER'S WELL
If you drink from this Well, Owyn can pick up UNFORTUNATE FLUX from the Tent at the end of the Trail. Otherwise the Tent will be empty.

 

TRAP
If you can kill the enemies, this Trap is wide open. Just make sure you don't stumble in front of a Blaster!

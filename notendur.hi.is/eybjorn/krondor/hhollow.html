TIP: If Lockpicking Skill is SELECTED (only), you will gain 7 points.

 

ISAAC
Isaac can be found in this sector throughout the game. He tends to wander, so his position varies between Chapters. His positions in Chapters 1, 2, 3, and 6 are indicated on the map above. Even if he's a scoundrel, he's also a mine of information, so don't forget to have a word with him if you're in the neighbourhood - he tends to come up with new tidbits every now and then, depending upon where you've been and what you've been doing. Isaac has the Ruby needed to solve the Quest of the Stolen Ruby (see LaMut Sector).

FRANKLIN'S HOUSE
  
In Chapter 1 the Franklin won't be here, but if you talk to him
in Chapters 2, 3 or 6, you can avoid fighting the 5 Rogues
North of Cavall Run (Combat 03).

 

AMBUSH BARN
 In Chapter 3 (only) the Barn contains six Moredhel Warriors waiting to ambush you. If you fight them, do enter the Barn afterwards. It's contents will differ drastically from the other Chapters. Note: If, in Chapter 3, you approach the Barn from the West, and your Scouting Skill is 45% or greater, you will spot some footprints going East.

Note also: One of the Moredhel is carrying a Spynote revealing how to find the Knight's Piece needed to enter Cavall Run.

 

TRAP (South)
Push leftmost Crystal once, diagonally, towards Left Blaster.
Push middle Crystal twice, diagonally, towards Far Right Blaster.
The way out is now clear, if you don't do something stupid.

 

TRAP (North)
Note that there is a Blaster at [4,1]. This is visible in Chapter 6, but in Chapters 1, 2 and 3 one of the characters will hide it from you. Pushing the Transparent Crystal diagonally to the right will trigger this Blaster, and effectively ruin the Pole Current. Now there's a clear way out between the Trap Poles.

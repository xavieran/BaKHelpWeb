Most Spynotes only contain information, and may be discarded as soon as they've been read (and digested). Only one Spynote (#21) is essential for finishing the game. One other can be essential (#10), depending on circumstances. A few Notes are relevant, and even essential, to certain sub-quests (#08, #14, #20, #24 and others). Many Spynotes, although not needed, can be extremely helpful (#22, #23, #31, #32, and others). Some Notes trigger events, mainly dialogues (e.g. #14, #25). One Spynote (#27) can only be bought, and another one (#15) is a reward for a Quest performed.

Below you will find a complete listing of all Notes. I have also included Notes which have been deleted from the game, because they can be found in the game's data files, and can be hacked into Inventory (using BaKHacker).

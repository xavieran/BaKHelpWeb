SPECIAL NOTE:
Apart from the Ambush Houses, most of the enemies here are Pantathians (as opposed to Panth Tiandns). They carry Spells, but for some reason they seem most reluctant to use them. They will cast HOCHO'S HAVEN every now and then, but the resulting shield is easily penetrated. Perhaps they are suffering from lack of Manna?

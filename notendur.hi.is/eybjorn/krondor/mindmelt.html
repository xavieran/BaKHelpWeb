MIND MELT
 [SPELL HOME] [ALPHA] [TABLE] [CATEGORY] [OWYN] [PATRUS]

Scroll Price = 400.0

Cost Range = 5 - 15
Damage = Cost * 3

Line of Sight = NO

House in Dencamp [1.01 only]

MIND MELT is a basic Damage Spell, causing Damage equivalent to three times the Cost. It is especially potent against Goblins and Witch Hags, who suffer Double Damage. There is a catch, though: This spell is only available in version 1.01 of the game (the original diskette version). In version 1.02 (CD-ROM, Free Download) it has been made unavailable.

Example: Cast MIND MELT at maximum Cost (15) at a Goblin. It will cause 90 points of Damage.

A couple of Enemies are totally Immune to this Spell, while a few suffer less or more than the normal Damage:

GOBLINS Damage x 2
WITCH HAGS Damage x 2
PANTH TIANDNS Damage x 1.5
TROLLS Damage x 1.5
DREADS Damage x 0.5
MAKALA Damage x 0.5
NETHERMANDERS IMMUNE
TSURANI SPELLWEAVERS IMMUNE

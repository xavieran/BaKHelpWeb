TRAP
This TRAP is pretty easy, but the raw graphics may confuse you. The middle Blaster points towards you and the Red Trap Poles. The character standing next to the Transparent Crystal should push it to trigger the Middle Blaster. The Red Poles will be ruined. Pass between them, and use the Solid Crystal to block the Left Blaster. Now the way out should be obvious.

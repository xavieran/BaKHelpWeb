THE GREEN NUMBERS
Here's an example (Two-Handed Broadsword):

Enemies: 276 - Caches: 15 - Shops:10
This means that 276 Enemies carry this type of Sword,
it can be found in 15 Caches, and 10 Shops have it for sale.

70 - 5 - 25
These three numbers are derived from the game's data files, and are all believed to be related to the dulling of Swords. The first number (00-100) and the second number (1-6) probably indicate the likelihood of a Sword being dulled, and the rate of dulling (by no means verified). High numbers seem to indicate rapid dulling. The third number is almost certainly the lowest percentage a Sword can reach.

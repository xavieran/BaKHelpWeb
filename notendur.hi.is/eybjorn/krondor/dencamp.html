PASSWORD HOUSE in DENCAMP
In a House at the end of the Dencamp North Trail some Soldiers are resting. They will demand a Password before they allow you to enter.
You can enter, if you're playing version 1.01 of the game, but if you're playing version 1.02, don't even try.

[1.01] Go back down the North Trail, and enter the House where the Trail joins the Main Road. A red-haired man will give you a password: "Golden Treasure". Return to the Soldiers' House, only to find that you've been given the wrong password! Go back to the red-haired man's house, and get the correct password: "Diamonds". The Soldiers will now admit you, and tell you all about poor Lionel, a Magician who "burned those Goblins' brains out with some spell", but whose spell "didn't work as well on the Troll that killed him." As you are about to leave, the Soldiers will remember a Scroll left by Lionel, and give it to you. This is the MIND MELT Spell, only available here (and very efficient against Goblins).

[1.02] The Soldiers will be in their House, but you can only visit the red-haired man once, and receive the wrong password. If you try to visit him again, he will have left. As a result you can not get MIND MELT in this version of the game. Don't ask me why, but it looks like the programmers did this intentionally.
 

HOUSE SOUTH OF DENCAMP
This is an ordinary House, if visited in Chapters 1, 2 or 3. In Chapter 5 (before Quest is active) do not approach it, unless you want to encounter a Magical Field, causing 5 point damage every time you run into it. Six Invisible Spellcasters are responsible, and they will only reveal themselves, if the relevant Quest is active (see Duke Martin in the Northwarden Sector for details).
  

DENCAMP BARN
Usually this is just an ordinary Barn, containing a few coins. In Chapter 5 it is more important. If you've activated the FIND TAMNEY QUEST, the renegade Minstrel will be found here. You will need a minimum of 30 Strength to open the Barn, and then Tamney will give you a new Quest to perform. See Duke Martin in the Northwarden Sector for details.
